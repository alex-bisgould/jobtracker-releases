Job Tracker for Mac

Your job search, organized — applications, contacts, interviews, and AI help, all on your own Mac.

Download for Mac

Requires an Apple Silicon Mac (M1 or later) · macOS 12+ · signed & notarized by Apple

First-time setup

  1. Open the downloaded Job-Tracker-mac-arm64.dmg.
  2. Drag Job Tracker into your Applications folder.
  3. Open it from Applications. If macOS ever asks, right-click the app and choose Open.

The app keeps itself up to date automatically — no need to return here.
